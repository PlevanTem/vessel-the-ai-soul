# Soul Miner — Content Source Priority

## Priority Tiers

### P0 — First-Hand Writing (Highest Value)
Every word directly written by the person, unmediated.

- Personal blog / website essays (full text)
- Newsletter issues (full text)
- Books (publicly available text)
- Long Twitter threads (original, not retweets)
- GitHub READMEs and documentation they authored
- Personal essays / op-eds

**Why P0**: Represents their deliberate, considered expression. Most likely to
capture their authentic voice and fully-formed ideas.

---

### P1 — Spoken Content (High Value)
Their own words, captured in audio/video, transcribed.

- Podcast episodes (as guest or host) — transcript
- Conference talks / TED talks — transcript
- YouTube videos / channels — subtitles
- Recorded interviews — transcript
- Lecture recordings — transcript

**Why P1**: Slightly less curated than writing, but captures natural speech
patterns, off-the-cuff thinking, and conversational voice. Very valuable for
Communication Style analysis.

---

### P2 — Short-Form Content (Accumulative Value)
Valuable in aggregate; each piece carries less signal.

- Twitter/X posts (original only, exclude retweets and replies to others)
- Reddit / Hacker News comments
- LinkedIn posts
- Short responses in interviews

**Why P2**: Individual posts are low signal, but 100+ posts create statistical
patterns in vocabulary, topics, stance. Treat as a corpus, not individual quotes.

---

### P3 — Secondary Sources (Use with Caution)
Content ABOUT the person, not BY them.

- News articles, profiles, biographies
- Others' summaries of their views
- Wikipedia (for factual context only, NOT for views)
- Book reviews

**When to use**: Only when P0-P2 is insufficient (< 3,000 words total).
**Never use** to attribute opinions — they may be paraphrased or taken out of context.
Always cross-reference with primary sources.

---

## Minimum Quality Thresholds

| Quality Level | P0+P1 Words | Action |
|---------------|-------------|--------|
| Excellent | > 30,000 | Full distillation, high confidence |
| Good | 10,000–30,000 | Full distillation, moderate confidence |
| Acceptable | 3,000–10,000 | Distillation with more [INFERRED] markers |
| Insufficient | < 3,000 | Pause, report, ask user for more sources |

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- ❌ Using aggregate bio pages (crunchbase, Wikipedia summary) for views
- ❌ Quoting out-of-context one-liners as definitive positions
- ❌ Treating satirical or hypothetical statements as sincere views
- ❌ Using content from others who "summarize" this person's philosophy
