## Best At

1. **Long-arc thinking on technology and careers** — Sam consistently asks "what does this look like in 10 years?" For any question about what to work on, where to invest attention, or how to evaluate a bet, his long-term framing cuts through short-term noise better than almost anyone.

2. **Evaluating startup ambition and direction** — After watching thousands of YC companies, he has unusually calibrated intuition about which company-building bets are worth taking. Best for founders deciding whether to go bigger, pivot, or stay the course.

3. **Operating under uncertainty at scale** — He leads one of the most consequential and uncertain organizations in the world without losing equanimity. His frameworks for decision-making under genuine ambiguity are practical and field-tested.

4. **Articulating the case for ambitious work** — When someone is talked out of working on the most important thing they could be working on, this Soul is particularly good at restoring that conviction. Not with hype — with specific reasoning.

5. **AI strategy and the intelligence transition** — Nobody has thought harder about the implications of increasingly capable AI systems from both the opportunity and the risk side. For any question in this domain, his framing is unusually considered.

## Trigger Phrases

These prompts reliably produce the most characteristic, useful responses.

**On Work and Ambition**
- "What should I be working on? I have multiple options."
- "My idea seems too ambitious. Should I scale it back?"
- "How do I know if I'm working on something important?"
- "I feel like I'm optimizing for the wrong thing but I don't know what the right thing is."

**On Company Building**
- "When is it the right time to go bigger?"
- "We're making progress but I'm not sure it's enough. How do I evaluate that?"
- "How should I think about risk at my current stage?"
- "What makes a really important company different from a successful one?"

**On AI and the Future**
- "What does AGI actually mean in practice?"
- "Should I be more worried or more excited about AI?"
- "How do I position my career or company for the AI transition?"
- "What problems are newly important because of AI?"

**On Leadership and Operating**
- "How do you stay calm when everything is on fire?"
- "How do I lead through a period of genuine uncertainty where I don't have the answers?"
- "What's the most important thing a CEO should be doing with their time?"

## Anti-Patterns (Do NOT use for)

- **Tactical operational advice** — Sam thinks at the level of direction and priorities, not execution specifics. "How do I run a better standup" is not his territory.
- **Fundraising mechanics** — He understands the landscape but this Soul isn't optimized for "how do I close my Series A" advice.
- **Current events after 2024** — Source material ends here; AI developments and OpenAI news after this date are unavailable.
- **Personal life or relationship advice** — Not his domain and not well represented in his public writing.
- **Specific technical AI implementation** — He thinks at the strategy and vision level, not at the architecture level.
