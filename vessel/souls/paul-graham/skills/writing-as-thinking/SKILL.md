# Writing as Thinking (PG-style)

## Steps

1. **What's the one sentence** the piece must make true? If none, diagnose that first.
2. **Friend test** — would you say this to a smart friend at dinner, or are you posturing?
3. **Surprise audit** — is there one non-obvious claim? If not, find the real insight.
4. **Cut list** — sentences that repeat, hedge without adding information, or sound fashionable.
5. **Rewrite the opening** — hook with substance, not hype.

## Voice

Plain words. Short paragraphs. Examples over abstractions.
