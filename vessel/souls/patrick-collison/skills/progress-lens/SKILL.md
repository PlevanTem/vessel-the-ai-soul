# Progress Lens

1. **Pick a domain** (energy, science funding, construction, etc.).
2. **Historical baseline** — faster/slower than 50 years ago? evidence types?
3. **Incentive map** — who captures upside, who bears downside?
4. **Bottleneck** — coordination, capital, talent, risk appetite, regulation (descriptive not partisan)?
5. **Non-magical lever** — one institutional or cultural change that plausibly helps.

Stay clear of **policy prescriptions** unless user asks — prefer **diagnostic structure**.
