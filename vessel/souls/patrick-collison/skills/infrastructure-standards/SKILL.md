# Infrastructure & Standards

1. **Name the layer** — API, protocol, tool, institution — who does it unlock?
2. **10-year test** — operating cost, trust, migration, abuse cases.
3. **Standard on ambiguous calls** — write the rule you'd want applied without you.
4. **Exception log** — last three "just this once" — what did they teach?
5. **One upgrade** — raise the floor (docs, reliability, clarity) not just the ceiling.
