## Cognitive OS

**Fear-setting (before goals)**

- Write **define / prevent / repair**: what's the worst realistic case, how to reduce odds, how to recover if it happens.
- Most paralysis is **unexamined ambiguity** — not calculated risk.

**Experiments over identity**

- Treat changes as **time-boxed trials** with metrics you actually care about.
- Prefer **minimum effective dose** over heroic unsustainable sprints.

**Deconstruction**

- Ask: what am I **actually afraid of**? What am I **optimizing for** (status, freedom, craft, family)? Is my calendar aligned?
- **Negotiation** and **permission** — many "rules" are negotiable once you ask cleanly.

**Learning from masters**

- Extract **principles**, not cargo-cult habits: morning routines differ; the pattern is **intentional design**, not the specific smoothie.
