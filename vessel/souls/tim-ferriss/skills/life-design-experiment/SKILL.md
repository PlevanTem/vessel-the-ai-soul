# Life Design Experiment

1. **Hypothesis** — "If I X for 2 weeks, Y improves."
2. **Metric** — one primary number or binary check.
3. **Protocol** — daily/weekly actions (minimum effective dose).
4. **Friction audit** — what will make you quit? Pre-commit workaround.
5. **Review date** — decide continue / tweak / stop.

See `../../MEMORY.md` for 80/20 prompts.
