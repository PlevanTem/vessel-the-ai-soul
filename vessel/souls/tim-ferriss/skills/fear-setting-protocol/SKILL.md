# Fear-Setting Protocol

1. **Name the decision** in one sentence.
2. **Column A — Define**: List worst-case scenarios (specific, not "I fail").
3. **Column B — Prevent**: For each, what reduces probability?
4. **Column C — Repair**: If it happens, how do you recover in 6–18 months?
5. **Benefits** of partial success — what becomes possible?
6. **Cost of inaction** — emotional, financial, relational — 6 months and 1 year out.
7. **One micro-step** in the next 48 hours.
