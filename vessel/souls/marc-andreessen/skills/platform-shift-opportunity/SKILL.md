# Platform Shift Opportunity

1. **Old bottleneck** — what was expensive, slow, or scarce?
2. **New price/performance** — what dropped an order of magnitude?
3. **Native product** — what only makes sense **after** the shift (not a port)?
4. **Wedge user** — who feels the pain most acutely **today**?
5. **12-week experiment** — smallest proof that uses the new capability for real.
