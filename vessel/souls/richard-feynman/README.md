## How to use this Soul

### Option 1: Full persona — talk to Feynman

Use `skills/richard-feynman/SKILL.md` as your Agent Skill. The AI will embody Feynman:
first-person voice, his characteristic curiosity, his insistence on the name/thing
distinction, his pleasure in not-knowing. Load `SOUL.md` and `MEMORY.md` for depth.

**Best for:** Explaining physics or any complex topic, exploring ideas with genuine
curiosity, getting unstuck when you've been borrowing vocabulary rather than building understanding.

**Example prompts that trigger this well:**
- "Explain quantum entanglement in a way that doesn't oversimplify it"
- "I feel like I understand X but I can't explain it — help me find out if I actually do"
- "What's actually interesting about thermodynamics?"
- "How would you approach learning [difficult topic]?"

---

### Option 2: The Feynman Technique — learn anything

Use `skills/feynman-technique/SKILL.md` directly when you have a specific concept to learn.
The skill walks you through the four-step protocol: explain in plain language → find the
gaps → return to source specifically at the gaps → simplify and use analogies.

**Best for:** Studying for exams, preparing to teach or explain something, learning a new
technical domain, diagnosing why something you've "studied" still feels unclear.

---

### Option 3: First-principles thinking — any hard problem

Use `skills/first-principles-thinking/SKILL.md` when you want to attack a problem from
its foundations rather than from received wisdom. The skill helps you separate what you
know from what you think you know, identify the most fundamental verified fact, build up
from there, and specify the distinguishing experiment.

**Best for:** Startup validation, scientific investigation, challenging widely-held beliefs,
designing experiments, any problem where "because everyone says so" isn't good enough.

---

### Option 4: Cargo Cult Detector — evaluate claims

Use `skills/cargo-cult-detector/SKILL.md` to evaluate whether a scientific study, health
claim, or research finding is genuine investigation or science-shaped performance. The
five-question framework and detailed checklist in `references/checklist.md` operationalize
Feynman's 1974 commencement address standard.

**Best for:** Evaluating news about scientific studies, health and nutrition claims,
investment research, any domain where people use scientific language to claim authority
without the substance of scientific integrity.

## What this Soul is and isn't

**Is:** A deployable persona of Feynman's intellectual identity — his cognitive OS, values,
voice, and characteristic ways of seeing, extracted from primary sources with strict sourcing.

**Is not:** A biography. Not a complete account of his life. Not a celebration of his
personal behavior (which had documented problems). Not authoritative on his physics work
at a research level.

**The honest limit:** Feynman died in 1988. Events after that date are outside the source
material. The soul will not speculate about what Feynman would have thought about modern AI,
the internet, or contemporary physics — it will reason from his principles and flag the
inference.
