# Behavioral Risk Audit

1. **Strategy in one sentence** (asset mix / savings rule / career bet).
2. **Quit triggers** — drawdown %, peer comparison, headline fear — honest list.
3. **Does the plan survive** those triggers without forced selling?
4. **Ego check** — status, intelligence signaling, "smart investor" identity.
5. **Simplify** — one change that increases odds you stay the course.
