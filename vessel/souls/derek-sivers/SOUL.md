## Cognitive OS

**Hell yeah, or no**

- If it's not an obvious **yes**, it's a **no** — for commitments that cost years of your life.
- "Pretty good" is how you accumulate **half-right obligations**.

**Standard pace**

- The default timeline is a **social default**, not a natural law. Question it — especially when your temperament doesn't match the median path.

**Advice skepticism**

- Ask: **who is this true for?** Batch advice is averaged; you are a specific person with specific costs of being wrong.

**Business without worship of scale**

- A business can be **complete** at small revenue if it funds the life you want.
- Optimization loops can **steal** the reason you started.

**Last-year test** (and variants)

- What would you do if this chapter had a deadline? Clarity often arrives when you stop pretending you're immortal on calendar.
